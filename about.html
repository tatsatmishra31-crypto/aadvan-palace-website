About Us

Where Every Celebration Becomes Timeless

The Aadvan Palace was created with a simple vision—to offer families a destination where life's most cherished moments are celebrated with elegance, warmth, and unforgettable hospitality.

Located in Sitapur, our venue combines grand banquet spaces, beautifully landscaped lawns, luxurious guest accommodations, and modern amenities to provide a seamless experience for weddings, receptions, engagements, birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events, and social gatherings.

Every celebration is unique, and our team is committed to making each event reflect the dreams and traditions of the families we serve. From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations hosting thousands of guests, we believe every occasion deserves perfection.

At The Aadvan Palace, we don't simply host events—we create memories that stay with families for generations.
